How to add/remove a logo


To add a logo or a thumbnail in the middle of the QR you just have to open the File menu and click on “Add Logo”. It will open a panel allowing you to choose the image file. Once selected, a little mark sign will appear next to “Add Logo” (in File menu) remembering you that a logo has been selected. If a QR has been created it will be updated with the logo. But if a QR hasn’t been created, don’t worry, the app will remember the logo and will apply it when you’ll generate the QR.



If you click again on “Add Logo” it will forget the logo chosen and the mark will disappear along the logo in the QR generated (if any).
